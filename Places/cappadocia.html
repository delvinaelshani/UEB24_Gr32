    Discover Cappadocia

    Cappadocia, Turkey offers a magical experience like no other. From its captivating hot air balloon rides over the fairy chimneys to its ancient cave churches, immerse yourself in a land of wonder and history.

    The "Blue Eye" is a water spring and natural phenomenon occurring in the village of Muzinë in Finiq municipality, southern Albania. A popular tourist attraction, the clear blue water of the river can be seen from a depth of more than fifty metres.
