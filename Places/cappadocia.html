Cappadocia

BeautyWonder offers special tours for Cappadocia, where history dances with the wind and dreams take flight amidst the ancient stones. A realm where the Earth itself becomes a canvas, painted with the vibrant hues of culture and adorned with the whispers of centuries past. In Cappadocia you can:

  • Embark on mesmerizing hot air balloon rides over the surreal landscapes.
  • Explore the ancient cave churches of Göreme Open-Air Museum.
  • Immerse yourself in the rich culture through local craft shops and markets.
  • Hike or horseback ride through the breathtaking valleys and trails.

Discover Cappadocia

Cappadocia, Turkey offers a magical experience like no other. From its captivating hot air balloon rides over the fairy chimneys to its ancient cave churches, immerse yourself in a land of wonder and history.

The "Blue Eye" is a water spring and natural phenomenon occurring in the village of Muzinë in Finiq municipality, southern Albania. A popular tourist attraction, the clear blue water of the river can be seen from a depth of more than fifty metres.
