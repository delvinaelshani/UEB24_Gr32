Cappadocia, Turkey

Cappadocia is renowned for its hot air balloon rides that offer breathtaking views of the surreal landscape during sunrise. The sight of hundreds of colorful balloons floating above the rocky terrain is truly magical.

Amalfi Coast, Italy

The Amalfi Coast in Italy is a stunning stretch of coastline along the Sorrentine Peninsula in the Campania region. Known for its dramatic cliffs, colorful villages, crystal-clear waters, and charming towns.
